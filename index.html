Welcome to Monkeys and Bananas

Rules:

1. Based on two players, player one goes first to click the dice button.
2. The number rolls and is displayed on the screen, the player moves to that number.
3. If a player lands on a banana they will move back a few spaces to the highlighted box.
4. If a player lands on a monkey they will move forward a few spaces to the highlighted box.
5. If there is neither a banana nor a monkey then player stays put and player 2 begins.
6. Once one player reaches square 49 they win the game.
7. A message will pop up to tell you if you have won.
7. If you wish to reset the game then the reset button is located next to the dice button and players will begin back at the beginning to start again.
